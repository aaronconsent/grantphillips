Booking

Request a date.

Tell me what you're planning and I'll reply within one business day with availability and a flat-rate quote. No deposit required to check the date.

Or email directly: booking@grantphillipsbass.com

What I'll need to confirm a date

  • Date, start time, and total time on site
  • Venue address (or "TBD — within 30 miles of X")
  • Style: solo, duo, trio, quartet — I can recommend collaborators
  • Any specific pieces (processional, hymns, jazz tunes)
  • Whether amplification is required

Typical lead time

Weddings: 3-12 months ideal, but I'll always check shorter dates.
Church services: 2-6 weeks.
Jazz / restaurant: 1-4 weeks.
Orchestral subs: as little as 48 hours.
