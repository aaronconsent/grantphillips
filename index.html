About Grant

A bassist trained in the orchestra, fluent in the standards.

Grant Dewell Phillips is a double bassist studying at the Michigan State University College of Music, where he is a graduate-level performer in the MSU Symphony Orchestra. He came up through Houston's Kinder HSPVA, earned back-to-back Texas All-State selections (Symphonic Band, 2018; Symphony Orchestra, 2019), and completed a dual major in astrophysics and orchestral music at the University of Michigan in the studio of Professor Nicholas Walker — a Fulbright Scholar and past president of the International Society of Bassists.

His sound is grounded and unhurried — equally at home behind a string quartet at a vineyard ceremony, anchoring a hymn at Easter Vigil, or walking changes in a Detroit jazz room.
